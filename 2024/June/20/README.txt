(VERSION 20)

All relevant information published for this update.

===== FIXES =====
- Fix for moving back and forth between reportable players & chat lines in the call for help flow.
- Fix for opening the Catalogue page, if attempting to purchase something from the Catalogue without having enough credits.
- Fixed "Habbo Hotel: Origins" being cut off in the Habbo Launcher.

===== KNOWN ISSUES =====
Features / client:
- Loading full rooms and clicking "cancel" sometimes disconnects the player.
- Sticky notes work intermittently, sometimes they refuse to open, particularly after having been active for a few hours.
- A small segment of players are not able to enter their own rooms in some cases.
- People are shown offline in the console, even though they are online.
- In navigator, "Hide Full Rooms" is not working, either it does nothing or just cycles between room capacity indicators.
- No error message is shown when trying to enter a full room, or when trying to enter a HC room without HC.
- Purse transactions are not loading.

Public room issues:
- Infobus poll texts and kiosk texts are not fully visible in some cases.
- InfoBus text shown when trying to enter the bus without HC shows and out of date text.
- The Oasis public room is faulty: can enter the bot's area, there is no bot present, and the entrance point is wrong.
- While diving in the Lido, everyone else in the room is stuck on the diving player's screen until they're finished.
- Avatars suddenly stop swimming in the Lido in some cases.
- You can't enter in the second room in The Chromide Club, it takes you to the Hotel View.
- Some public room furni that are supposed to give hand items to the players are not giving anything.
- You lose a game ticket if you queue in Wobble Squabble and then leave before your turn.

Catalogue:
- Players are unable to purchase some items from the Catalogue.
- Navigating to another Catalogue page while another one is loading gets the game stuck at infinite "Loading..." until you close and reopen the Catalogue.
- Roller packs (15c and 25c) don't clarify how many are included.

Localisations / links:
- Some item names are reported to be wrong.
- Some public rooms and items have outdated names and descriptions.
- Some links point to outdated pages.
- In-game links open in Edge or Internet Explorer (or other browsers), overriding the default browser that is set.
